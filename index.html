What types of actuators are manufactured in Mexico?

Mexico manufactures linear, hydraulic, pneumatic, electric, rotary, and valve actuators. Major manufacturing clusters exist in Nuevo Leon, Queretaro, and Guanajuato, with deep supply chains built around automotive, aerospace, and electronics OEMs.

Are Mexican actuators USMCA compliant?

Yes. Actuators manufactured in Mexico qualify for USMCA duty-free treatment when they meet the 75% regional value content threshold. The 2026 USMCA review may increase these thresholds, making it critical to source from verified North American manufacturers now.
